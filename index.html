Feed Eggs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I feed eggs?

To feed the egg man eggs, simply use your finger to drag an egg from the basket to the egg man's mouth.

Why am I experiencing such a great time?

This game combines intuitive drag-and-drop mechanics with delightful animations, challenging players to collect eggs by dragging them from the basket to the egg man, offering a captivating and rewarding experience for all office workers.
